About Our Clinic

Holistic Healing Since 2010

Natucure Acupuncture & Herbal Medicine Clinic was founded with a vision to bring authentic Traditional Chinese Medicine to our community. Our practitioner has over 15 years of experience in acupuncture, herbal medicine, and holistic healing.

We believe in treating the root cause of illness, not just the symptoms. Our integrated approach combines ancient wisdom with modern understanding to help you achieve optimal health and wellness.

At Natucure, we create personalized treatment plans for each patient, considering your unique constitution, lifestyle, and health goals. We're committed to providing compassionate care in a peaceful, healing environment.

Meet Our Practitioner

Dong Li, L.Ac., Dipl. O.M.

Licensed Acupuncturist & Herbal Medicine Specialist

With over 15 years of clinical experience, Dong Li is a highly skilled practitioner dedicated to providing exceptional care through Traditional Chinese Medicine. She holds a Master's degree in Traditional Chinese Medicine and is board-certified in Oriental Medicine.

Dong specializes in:

  • Chronic pain management and musculoskeletal disorders
  • Stress reduction and emotional wellness
  • Digestive health and autoimmune conditions
  • Women's health and hormonal balance
  • Preventive wellness and longevity

Her approach combines meticulous diagnosis with compassionate care, ensuring each treatment is tailored to your unique needs. Dong believes in empowering patients through education and collaborative healing.
